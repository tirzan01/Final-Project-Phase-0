About us

This is a traditional Italian pizza restaurant. Here we have the honor to work with some excellent pizza maker who make pizza not for job but for passion. We have within our staff some valuable and proffessional chef who have started to cook Italian dishes for as long as they can remember, and they are able to prove this by showcasing it on the dish they are making. Originally from Puglia, in south Italy, our shop is located in Sydney, however, once you walk in, you will feel like taking a trip to Italy, with the love, charisma and food that are tipical of our beloved Italian culture.

Menu

Straight from South Italy, our menu offers a variety of traditional dishes. Our menu is carefully designed to match anyone taste. We work with fresh ingredients, and our dishes our made daily. Our menu is designed with only one goal: make evryone see that the sentence:'Italian food is the best worldwide!' is the truth and not just a stereotype
